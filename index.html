Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

In short

Flusii is a personal budgeting app. Your financial data lives on your device. We don’t sell your data, we don’t run ad trackers, and our AI never receives information that identifies you. We earn money only from the optional subscription — not from your data.

Who we are

Flusii (“we”, “us”) is the data controller for the limited processing described below. For privacy questions, contact us at abdulalhawani@gmail.com.

What data the app handles

  • Financial data you enter — incomes, expenses, budgets, goals, accounts, balances, receipts and photos you add. This is stored locally on your device.
  • Profile basics — the name, and optionally email/city you provide, used to personalise the app (e.g. the greeting). Used locally.
  • Account (optional) — if you sign in with Apple or Google to enable cloud backup, your email and a user identifier are processed by our authentication provider to secure your backup.
  • Diagnostics — basic, non-identifying technical information needed to keep the app working.

Flusii AI — how it protects your identity

When you use the AI features, the app sends only the text needed to answer your question to our secure AI service. Before it leaves your phone, identifying details (such as your name and city) are removed, and our server additionally redacts emails, account numbers and long digit sequences before the request reaches the AI model. The AI therefore does not know who you are. Requests are transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS).

Cloud backup (optional)

If you choose to sign in, your data can be backed up to the cloud (via Google Firebase and/or your iCloud) so you can restore it on a new device. This is entirely optional — the app works fully without an account, keeping everything on-device. You can sign out and delete your data at any time.

Bank data

Flusii does not connect to your bank. Account balances are entered manually, or imported by you from a CSV file you export from your own online bank. We never see your bank login.

Payments

The optional Premium subscription is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or store your card details. Apple’s processing is governed by Apple’s privacy policy.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or third-party ad networks.
  • We do not send your identity to the AI provider.

Legal basis (GDPR)

We process the limited data above to provide the app you asked for (performance of contract) and, where relevant, based on your consent (e.g. cloud backup when you sign in). You can withdraw consent at any time by signing out and deleting your data.

International transfers

Some optional services (cloud backup, AI processing) may involve providers based outside the EU/EEA, including the United States. Where that happens, transfers are covered by appropriate safeguards (such as the EU–US Data Privacy Framework and/or Standard Contractual Clauses).

Data retention & your rights

On-device data stays until you delete it. You can reset and erase all data from inside the app at any time. Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or port your data, and to object to processing. To exercise these rights, contact abdulalhawani@gmail.com.

Children

Flusii is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The “last updated” date above reflects the current version.
